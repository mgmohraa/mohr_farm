Meet the Mohr Family

Mohr Farms has been a proud family-run cattle operation in Iowa for over 40 years. Founded by Larry Mohr, the farm is now a true family business with his sons Joe and Chris carrying forward the legacy with passion and care.

Larry Mohr

Larry is the heart and soul of Mohr Farms. Starting the farm from scratch over four decades ago, Larry’s wisdom and leadership keep the farm running strong as the family patriarch.

Joe Mohr

Joe is Larry’s oldest son, preparing to take over the day-to-day farming operations. His expertise in sustainable cattle practices and machinery keeps the farm innovative and efficient.

Chris Mohr

Chris, the youngest son, recently joined the family business full time. His fresh ideas and energy complement the family’s strong farming traditions perfectly.
