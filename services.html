What We Offer

At Mohr Farms, we offer a variety of services built on generations of experience and a deep respect for the land and livestock. We proudly serve our community with premium cattle and friendly service.

Premium Angus Beef

We raise top-quality, locally grown Angus beef using sustainable practices. Available in custom orders or bulk packages.

Farm Tours

Experience Iowa farm life first-hand. Tour the barns, meet our cattle, and enjoy the fresh country air.

Custom Farming Work

We offer equipment and labor for custom baling, seeding, and hay work for neighboring farms in the region.

Family Education

We host small events to educate local families and youth on agriculture, sustainability, and raising livestock responsibly.
